    To submit a job: qsub <exec_script_with_our_job>
– You get back a job_ID
– Example: qsub test.sh
– For instance we get “293409”

    To monitor a job: qstat <job_ID>
– You get back job status info
– Example: qstat 293409

    To cancel a job: qdel <job_ID>
– Action job cancelled
– Example: qdel 293409
